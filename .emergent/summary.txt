<product_requirements>
Fiquant TaxPro is a Nigerian tax management solution for 2026 tax laws, aimed at individuals and businesses. It features Personal Income Tax (PAYE) and Corporate Income Tax (CIT) calculators, with PAYE supporting reliefs and progressive tax bands, and CIT handling various financial components like turnover, expenses, and loss relief. A bulk PAYE calculator allows  Excel template download (watermarked) and upload. The application's UI/UX emphasizes a professional, black and gold Fiquant Consult brand aesthetic, utilizing modern Shadcn UI components, responsive design, and consistent Naira symbol (₦) for currency.

A secure User Accounts & Profiles system, featuring email/phone verification, enables personalized history and future monetization. An Administrative Dashboard is required, accessible via a separate  portal (initially for super admins only). This dashboard manages user accounts (view, edit, suspend, roles, audit trail), provides real-time user analytics (active users, calculator usage, churn, exportable reports), tracks all admin actions in audit logs (with IP/user agent), and monitors system health. Admin accounts require 2FA, and the dashboard must align with the existing black/gold theme.

Further requirements included adding Name of Staff/Taxpayer, Month, and State of Residence fields to the PAYE calculator and reports, and Year of Assessment and Tax Year fields to the CIT calculator and reports. Comprehensive Terms and Conditions, absolving Fiquant Consult of liability for tax accuracy, were needed, along with a disclaimer footnote on calculator screens and a checkbox for T&Cs acceptance (later moved to login/registration screens). An email service integration was specified for automated notifications (verification, password reset, tax reminders) and admin broadcasts, with template management, user preferences, and analytics. Lastly, thorough tax information based on the New Nigeria Tax Act (NTA) 2026 and associated laws was requested, and the calculation history tab needs reformatting for explorability and printing.
</product_requirements>
