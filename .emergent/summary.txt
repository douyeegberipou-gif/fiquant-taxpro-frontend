<product_requirements>
The goal is to build Fiquant TaxPro, a tax management application for individuals and businesses in Nigeria to calculate, manage, and comply with the new 2026 tax laws. The app should provide seamless tax computation, reminders, history logs, and compliance dashboards.

**Key Features Implemented/Requested:**
1.  **Personal Income Tax (PAYE) Calculator**:
    *   Apply reliefs: Consolidated Relief Allowance (CRA), pension (8%), NHF (2.5%), life insurance, rent relief (capped at ₦500,000), NHIS premiums, health insurance.
    *   Auto-annualization of reliefs.
    *   Apply progressive tax bands (0% for first ₦800,000, then 15%, 18%, 21%, 23%, 25%).
    *   Output: Gross income, reliefs, taxable income, PAYE due, net pay.
    *   **Bulk PAYE Calculator (Spreadsheet Mode)**: A second option for businesses to calculate PAYE for multiple staff in a spreadsheet format, including all relief and deduction fields.
    *   **Excel Template Download/Upload**: Feature to download an Excel template, fill staff details, and upload for bulk computation. The template must be watermarked Fiquant Consult and be a true Excel file (.xlsx).

2.  **Corporate Income Tax (CIT) Calculator**:
    *   Input: Turnover, expenses (tag deductible vs. non-deductible).
    *   Apply rates: Small (0%), Medium (20%), Large (30%) companies.
    *   Apply thin-capitalization rule (interest deduction limits).
    *   **Capital Allowances Computation**: Inclusion of annual allowances for various asset classes.
    *   **Withholding Tax (WHT) Credits**: Field for user to input available WHT credits to arrive at Net Tax Payable.
    *   Output: Taxable profit, CIT due, deadlines.

3.  **UI/UX & Branding**:
    *   Dashboard-style UI.
    *   Professional branding with Fiquant Consult logo. Iterative refinement requested a black and gold aesthetic for the header, white lettering for Fiquant, gold for TaxPro, and the gold geometric triangular logo itself against the dark background.
    *   Modern design using Shadcn UI components.
    *   Responsive design.

4.  **Information & Compliance Tabs**:
    *   Tax Brackets tab displaying 2026 PAYE brackets.
    *   History tab for previous calculations.
    *   Tax Info tab with PAYE, CIT, and WHT information and rules.
    *   Compliance tab with PAYE, CIT, and WHT compliance deadlines and requirements.

5.  **Currency**: All currency values across the app should use the Naira symbol (₦).
</product_requirements>
