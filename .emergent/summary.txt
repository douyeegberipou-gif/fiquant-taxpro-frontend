<product_requirements>
Fiquant TaxPro is a Nigerian tax management solution for 2026 tax laws, targeting individuals and businesses. It offers PAYE and CIT calculators, with PAYE supporting reliefs and progressive tax bands, and CIT handling financial components like turnover and loss relief. A bulk PAYE calculator allows Excel template download and upload. The UI/UX features a professional black and gold Fiquant Consult brand, using Shadcn UI, responsive design, and consistent Naira symbol (₦).

User accounts include email/phone verification. An Administrative Dashboard (super admin only) manages users, provides analytics, and tracks audit logs. New fields Name of Staff/Taxpayer, Month, State of Residence for PAYE, and Year of Assessment, Tax Year for CIT were required. Comprehensive Terms and Conditions, a calculator disclaimer footnote, and T&Cs acceptance (moved to login/registration) were implemented. An email service for notifications and admin broadcasts was planned. Detailed Nigerian tax information (NTA 2026, TIN) was added.

Recently, the History tab required enhancement for interactivity and print options, and extraneous WHT information needed removal from it (but re-addition to Tax Info). New tax calculators for Value Added Tax (VAT), Payments Processing (for WHT and VAT), and Capital Gains Tax (CGT) were requested with specific logic (e.g., VAT transaction-based classification, updated CGT rates), print functions, disclaimers, and new input fields. A Home landing page with a hero section, branded banners for each tax type (with CTA navigation), and custom images was also required. Finally, the application needs to be optimized for mobile devices.
</product_requirements>
